UNDERSTAND YOUR WELL-BEING

When the application goes live, we'll ask a few simple questions so we can better understand your current level of well-being.

2

PERSONALISED INTERMITTENT FASTING

We'll then mentor you on how to achieve a successful Intermittent Fasting programme – starting the fasting, breaking the fast, nutrient absorption, duration of the fast, body type fast, what to absorb during fast.
